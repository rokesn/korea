└─────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────┘

  Status: ❌ CANNOT AUTO-FIX — Requires domain registration + DNS change

  Problem:
    Domain: translatebahasa.pro
    Content: 100% Korean-language Korean streaming/torrent link aggregator

  The domain name "translatebahasa" literally means "translate language" in
  Malay/Indonesian. This creates a catastrophic mismatch:
  • Google's language/geo classifier will be CONFUSED
  • Zero brand equity with Korean users
  • Korean users seeing translatebahasa.pro in search results will have
    low click-through confidence (-30% to -50% estimated CTR impact)

  Manual Fix Required:
    Step 1: Register a Korean-branded domain (e.g., jusomoа.kr, juso-moa.com)
    Step 2: Set up 301 redirects from translatebahasa.pro to new domain
    Step 3: Update SITE_URL in src/lib/site.ts  (single line change)
    Step 4: All other URLs update automatically (they use SITE_URL constant)
    Step 5: Re-submit sitemap to Google Search Console under the new domain
    Step 6: Register on 네이버 서치어드바이저 (searchadvisor.naver.com)
